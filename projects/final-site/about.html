About Matthew!

Matthew is currently a senior at Michigan State University. He is pursuing a Bachelor of Science (BS) degree in Computer Science, and is expecting to graduate in May 2013. Matthew has had a strong interest in technology since he was young because of the endless possibilities as to what you can develop. Because of this interest, Matthew hopes to get a full-time position in the software engineering field after graduation.

Matthew is currently employed at the Office of International Studies and Programs here at Michigan State University. He works as a student web administrator, and is responsible for managing and maintaining numerous sub-domains. In his work, he focuses on developing administrative web applications, which interface with MySQL databases and MongoDB databases, using the Zend PHP Framework.


When Matthew isn't busy with classes or work, he enjoys spending time with his pets and friends. He has a two year old kitten named Isabelle, and a two year old rabbit named Sydney. When hanging out with his friends, he likes to play video games and watch TV. Matthew recently purchased Halo 4, but he isn't the most skilled player.

Matthew also likes to spend time reading online magazines such as Wired. The constant advances in technology continue to amaze him, and only help to feed his interest. However, he also loves to travel! Matthew has been on numerous trips spanning across the United States. One of his favorite trips that he has taken was a trip to the New England, where he learned all about the Civil War.
