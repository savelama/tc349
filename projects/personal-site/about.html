Professional Bio

Matthew Savela is currently a senior at Michigan State University. He is currently pursuing a degree in Computer Science, and is drawn to software development because of the constant improvements made to technology and the endless possibilities those advances have to offer. He currently works as a student web administrator for the Office of International Studies and Programs. Matt has been recognized on Michigan State's Dean's List. In his spare time, you'll find Matt playing computer role-playing games such as Diablo 3 and World of Warcraft. Find Matt on Twitter at @savelama.
